01/07: Introduction to Digital Scholarly Editing, Dirk Van Hulle

Definitions:

  • Text: series of words and pauses recorded in a doc
  • Work: message or xp implied by
  • Document: physical vessel that contains or encarnates the text
  • Version: form of the work the the author intended at some particular time

Textual Criticism concerned with the way text is transmited over time and how transmission may inform our reading of the text; Scholarly Editing é output format

The European Society for Textual Scholarship
